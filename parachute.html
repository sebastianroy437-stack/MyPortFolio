REFLECTION

The flower exercise helped me reflect on my strengths, values, and career goals as an HR professional. It showed me that I work best in inclusive and supportive environments where collaboration, fairness, and employee well-being are valued. I also realized how important my skills in communication, conflict resolution, and structured planning are in building strong employee relations.

This activity further reminded me that my ultimate aim is to become a trusted partner in enhancing workplace culture and contributing to long-term organizational success. It also emphasized the need for continuous professional growth while maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Overall, the exercise gave me clarity about the kind of role and environment where I can thrive and make the most impact.
